Academic Programs

WCLU Fall 2025

Worldwide Christian Leadership University (WCLU) offers a range of academic programs designed to equip students for impactful service and leadership within the Christian community. The university is committed to combining academic excellence with spiritual growth, empowering students to become effective leaders for God’s mission.

Programs are delivered online via Zoom and Microsoft Teams, making education accessible for learners around the world. Courses are rooted in biblical principles and practical ministry experience, preparing students for real-world ministry, counseling, leadership, and teaching.

“Training men and women for God’s service through faith-based excellence and leadership education.”
  • Faith-centered academic excellence
  • Online and accessible learning
  • Practical leadership and ministry preparation
  • Integration of theology and real-world impact

Our Esteemed Faculty

WCLU Faculty Board 16 Professors

WCLU’s faculty members are a distinguished group of educators and professionals who combine academic achievement with real-world ministry experience. Each instructor is selected for their expertise, integrity, and alignment with WCLU’s mission of developing global Christian leaders.

Most of our faculty hold terminal degrees in their respective fields and bring years of professional and pastoral experience to the classroom. They are dedicated to academic freedom, open discussion, and the pursuit of excellence in faith and learning.

Registrar Office

136-4 Forum Drive, Suite 320, Columbia SC 29229 Tue - Thu | 12:00 PM - 3:00 PM (EST)

The Office of the Registrar at WCLU provides essential academic support services to students, faculty, and staff. It serves as the central hub for maintaining academic records, managing class registration, and ensuring smooth administrative operations.

Students can reach out to the Registrar for assistance with course enrollment, transcripts, and academic guidance. The office is managed by Arnitta Monk, who oversees the administration of student records and academic scheduling.

  • Email: info@wclu.org

  • Office Hours: Tuesday - Thursday, 12 PM - 3 PM (EST)
  • Registrar: Arnitta Monk
